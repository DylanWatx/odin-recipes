Blondies

Chocolate chip blondies

Blondies are like brownies but without the chocolate. Instead they are made with vanilla, brown sugar, and of course chocolate chips. Blondies are a great alterative to brownies if your not a fan of the overpowering chocolateyness of brownies. They are great on their own or they go perfectly with vanilla ice cream.

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 t backing powder
  • 0.5 t table salt
  • 12 T melted butter
  • 1.5 cups lightly packed brown sugar
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 4 t vanilla
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  1. Melt butter in a large mixing bowl
  2. Mix brown sugar with melted butter
  3. Whisk beaten eggs into the butter and brown sugar
  4. Add vanilla into butter, egg, and brown sugar mix
  5. Mix baking powder, salt and flour in a separate bowl
  6. Fold dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, be careful to not over mix
  7. Fold in chocolate chips
  8. Line a 9x13 inch pan with foil and lightly spray with cooking oil
  9. Evenly spread batter into pan
  10. Cook for 22-25 minutes or until surface is a light golden brown
