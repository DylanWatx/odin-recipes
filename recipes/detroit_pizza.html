Detroit Style Pizza

Detroit style pizza is the best if you like a nice crispy crust and lots of gooey cheese

Detroit pizza has the best NY pizza and Chicago pizza. It has a light crispy crust like New York pizza and the thick crust an edge toppings. What more could you want?

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 0.25 oz active dry yeast
  • 1 t sugar
  • 3 t olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2.5 cup bread flour

Toppings:

  • 8 oz shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 8 oz mild Cheddar cheese
  • 8 oz thick pepperoni
  1. Preheat oven to 550 degrees F
  2. Mix warm water in a mixer with yeast and sugar
  3. Add 2 t olive oil, salt, and bread flour
  4. Knead with a dough hook until smooth, soft, and elastic
  5. Pour remaining 1 t of olive oil into a 10x14 inch rectagular deep dish pan
  6. Put dough in pan , oil fingers, and pull dough to the edges of the pan
  7. Cover dough for a hour and leave it in room warm-room temp space
  8. Shredd cheeses
  9. After the hour is up pull dough to edge of pan, leaving about a half inch of space around the sides
  10. Put half of pepperoni on dough, followed by cheese, rest of pepperoni, and finally put 3 stripes of sause long ways across the pizza
  11. Bake for about 15 minutes or until golden brown and let cool for 5 mintues
  12. Carefully lift pizza out of pan with spatulla on to cutting board
