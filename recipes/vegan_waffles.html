Vegan Waffles

Vegan waffles with strawberries and blue berries

These are the BEST Vegan Waffles! They are crispy, so fluffy, and very simple with only pantry ingredients!

  • 6 T water
  • 2 T flax seed meal
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1.75 cup soy milk
  • 0.5 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 T canola oil
  • 4 t baking powder
  • 1t vanilla extract
  • 1 T agave nectar
  • 0.5 t salt
  1. Preheat your waffle maker
  2. Stir water and flax seed meal together in a bowl
  3. Blend oats in a blender into a flour-like consistency. .
  4. Add flax seed mixture, soy milk, all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, canola oil, baking powder, vanilla extract, agave nectar, and salt to oats
  5. Blend until the batter is slightly lumpy
